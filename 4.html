TIL - The Great Loop is a looped waterway exclusive tour of US and Canada

Published: January 8, 2023

The Great Loop stretches to roughly 9,700 km roughly and tours around the Eastern US and Canada going through natural and man-made navigable waterways. It can take a year or more to complete the entire loop.
