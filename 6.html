TIL - Leopard seals are the 3rd largest type of seal

Published: January 13, 2023

Elephant seals and walruses are the only two seals larger than them. The pups of a leopard seal are born 1.5 meters in size (5ft).

They live mostly in the subantarctic islands and antarctica.

They are the only seals whose diet consist of warm blooded animals, feasting on penguins and other seals.

They also consume krill, fish and cephalopods.

They lack teeth like lions and other land predators, as such rely on dragging their prey into the water trashing and tearing them apart for bite sized consumption.

They can speeds of around 40 km/h.

Their only recognised predators are the orca.
